# Course 204: Deep Learning Essentials

**Track:** Machine Learning Fundamentals
**Duration:** 4-5 hours
**Prerequisites:** Course 203
**Status:** Not Started

## What You'll Learn

By the end of this course you will:
- Understand why depth matters in neural networks — and the problems it creates
- Know what vanishing and exploding gradients are and how to address them
- Apply batch normalization and dropout correctly
- Understand residual connections and why they enable very deep networks
- Have a working overview of CNNs and when to use them
- Have a working overview of RNNs and their limitations
- Understand why transformers replaced RNNs

## Prerequisites

- Course 203: Neural Networks from Scratch

## Course Content

Why depth matters, vanishing/exploding gradients, batch normalization, dropout, residual connections, CNNs overview, RNNs overview, why transformers replaced RNNs.

## Who This Is For

Developers who have built a neural network from scratch and are ready to understand the architectural innovations that made modern deep learning possible — and that ultimately led to transformers and LLMs.

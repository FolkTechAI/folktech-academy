# Course 303: Fine-Tuning Models

**Track:** Applied AI Development
**Duration:** 5-6 hours
**Prerequisites:** Course 203 + 301
**Status:** Not Started

## What You'll Learn

By the end of this course you will:
- Know when fine-tuning is the right choice vs prompt engineering or RAG
- Prepare and format training data correctly
- Understand LoRA and QLoRA and why they make fine-tuning accessible
- Fine-tune a model on consumer hardware
- Evaluate a fine-tuned model against a baseline
- Recognize and diagnose common fine-tuning failure modes

## Prerequisites

- Course 203: Neural Networks from Scratch
- Course 301: Prompt Engineering & System Design

## Course Content

When to fine-tune vs prompt engineer vs RAG, preparing training data, LoRA and QLoRA, training on consumer hardware, evaluating fine-tuned models, common failure modes.

## Who This Is For

Developers who need a model to behave in a very specific way that prompting alone cannot achieve — and who want to do it efficiently without requiring expensive GPU clusters.

# Course 004: Local AI for Non-Developers

**Track:** AI for Everyone
**Duration:** 2-3 hours
**Prerequisites:** Course 002
**Status:** Not Started

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course you will:
- Understand what "running AI locally" means practically
- Have installed Ollama on your computer
- Have downloaded and run a local AI model
- Have had a conversation with AI running entirely on your machine

## Topics

1. What "local AI" means and why it matters
2. Privacy, cost, and offline benefits
3. Installing Ollama (step-by-step with screenshots)
4. Downloading your first model
5. Running a conversation locally
6. Troubleshooting common issues

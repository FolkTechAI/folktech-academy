# Course 002: AI Vocabulary You Need to Know

**Track:** AI for Everyone
**Duration:** 1 hour
**Prerequisites:** Course 001
**Status:** Not Started

## What You'll Learn

By the end of this course you will:
- Know the essential AI terms and what they mean in plain language
- Have a reference card you can keep and look up terms as needed
- Understand why these terms matter, not just what they mean

## Prerequisites

- Course 001: What AI Actually Is

## Course Content

Plain-language glossary covering: model, parameter, token, prompt, inference, training, fine-tuning, embedding, context window, hallucination, temperature, quantization, open weights, LLM, multimodal, GPU, CPU, RAM.

Each term includes:
- One-sentence definition
- Real-world analogy
- "Why it matters" note

## Who This Is For

Non-technical learners who want a solid vocabulary foundation before using AI tools or following technical conversations. Ideal after completing Course 001.

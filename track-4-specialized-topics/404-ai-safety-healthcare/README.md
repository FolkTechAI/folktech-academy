# Course 404: AI Safety for Healthcare

**Track:** Specialized Topics
**Duration:** 3-4 hours
**Prerequisites:** Course 301
**Status:** Not Started

## What You'll Learn

By the end of this course you will:
- Understand how hallucination presents uniquely dangerous risks in clinical contexts
- Implement safety sentinel patterns for AI outputs in healthcare
- Design escalation protocols for AI-assisted clinical workflows
- Apply HIPAA considerations to AI system design
- Follow responsible AI disclosure practices
- Learn from documented real-world healthcare AI failures

## Prerequisites

- Course 301: Prompt Engineering & System Design

## Course Content

Hallucination in clinical contexts, safety sentinel patterns, escalation protocols, HIPAA considerations for AI, responsible AI disclosure, real-world healthcare AI failures and lessons.

## Who This Is For

Developers and product teams building AI into healthcare workflows. Also relevant to clinical informatics teams evaluating AI tools. Anyone who needs to understand not just what can go wrong, but how to design systems that fail safely.

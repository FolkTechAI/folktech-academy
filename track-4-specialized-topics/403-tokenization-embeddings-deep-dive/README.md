# Course 403: Tokenization & Embeddings Deep Dive

**Track:** Specialized Topics
**Duration:** 2-3 hours
**Prerequisites:** Course 102
**Status:** Not Started

## What You'll Learn

By the end of this course you will:
- Understand the tradeoffs between character, word, and subword tokenization
- Know how the BPE (Byte Pair Encoding) algorithm works
- Understand SentencePiece and how it differs from BPE
- Explore embedding spaces and understand what they encode
- Run word similarity and analogy tasks
- Visualize high-dimensional embeddings in 2D

## Prerequisites

- Course 102: Inside the Neural Network

## Course Content

Character vs word vs subword tokenization, BPE algorithm, SentencePiece, exploring embedding spaces, word similarity and analogy tasks, visualizing embeddings in 2D.

## Who This Is For

Developers and ML practitioners who want a deep understanding of how text becomes numbers — and what those numbers actually represent. Essential for anyone working on tokenizer-sensitive problems (long context, multilingual, or domain-specific text).

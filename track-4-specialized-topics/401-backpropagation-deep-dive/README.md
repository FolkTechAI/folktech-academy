# Course 401: Backpropagation Deep Dive

**Track:** Specialized Topics
**Duration:** 2 hours
**Prerequisites:** Course 203
**Status:** Not Started

## What You'll Learn

By the end of this course you will:
- Understand the chain rule visually and mathematically
- Compute gradients by hand for a small network
- Build and read computational graphs
- Understand how automatic differentiation works under the hood
- Diagnose common training failures by tracing gradient flow

## Prerequisites

- Course 203: Neural Networks from Scratch

## Course Content

Chain rule explained visually, computing gradients by hand for a small network, computational graphs, automatic differentiation, understanding training failures.

## Who This Is For

Developers and ML practitioners who want a rigorous understanding of backpropagation beyond "it computes gradients." Ideal for anyone who has hit a training failure and wants to know why it actually happened.

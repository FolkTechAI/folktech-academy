# Course 402: Attention & Transformers Deep Dive

**Track:** Specialized Topics
**Duration:** 3-4 hours
**Prerequisites:** Course 203
**Status:** Not Started

## What You'll Learn

By the end of this course you will:
- Implement self-attention from scratch and understand every step
- Extend that to multi-head attention and understand why multiple heads help
- Understand positional encoding and positional embeddings
- Trace through the full transformer block (attention + FFN + normalization)
- Implement a mini transformer in Python

## Prerequisites

- Course 203: Neural Networks from Scratch

## Course Content

Self-attention from scratch, multi-head attention, positional encoding/embedding, the full transformer block, implementing a mini transformer in Python.

## Who This Is For

Developers and researchers who want to understand the transformer architecture at the level needed to modify, fine-tune, or build on top of it — not just use it as a black box.
